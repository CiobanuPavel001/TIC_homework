Poezie

Afara-i toamna

  de Mihai Eminescu
_______________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________

Afara-i toamna, frunza -mprastiata,,
iarvantul zvarle-n geamuri grele picuri,
Situ citesti scrisori din roase plicuri,
Si intr-un ceas gandesti la viata toata..

Pierzandu-titimpul tau cu dulci nimicuri
N-aivrea ca nime-n usa ta sa bata;,
Darsi mai bine-i,cand afara-i zloata
Sastai visand la foc,de somn sapicuri..
